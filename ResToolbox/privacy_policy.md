# ResToolbox3 Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 6, 2026

## Overview

ResToolbox3 ("the App") is a reservoir engineering calculation tool developed by Mark Burgoyne. Your privacy is important, and this policy explains how the App handles your data.

## Data Collection

The App does **not** collect, store, transmit, or process any personal information or user data. Specifically:

- No personal information (name, email, phone number, address, etc.) is collected.
- No usage analytics or telemetry data is collected.
- No device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data are collected.
- No cookies, tracking pixels, or similar technologies are used.
- No user accounts or login credentials are required.

## Data Storage

All calculations performed within the App are processed entirely on your device. No data is stored on external servers or cloud services. The App does not maintain any databases of user information.

## Data Sharing

Since no data is collected, no data is shared with any third parties. The App does not:

- Share or sell any user data to third parties.
- Integrate with third-party analytics or advertising services.
- Transmit any information over the internet.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services that collect user data.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
